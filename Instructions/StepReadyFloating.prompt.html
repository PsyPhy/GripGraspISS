Ready to Start

  • Adopt the quasi-freefloating posture in the Floating Backrest.

  • Attach Chest Marker Support to your torso and verify that it is well centered left-to-right on your chest.

  • Place the Virtual Reality Headset on your forehead in preparation for performing science tasks.

  • Attach the Hand Marker Support to your right hand and attach strap on cable to forearm near elbow.

  • Take the VR Headset Remote in your left hand.

Pull the Virtual Reality Headset over your eyes then press the Return Button on the VR Headset Remote to begin.
